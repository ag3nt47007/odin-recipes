Tiramisu

A coffee-flavored italian dessert

One of the best and easiest italian desserts ever

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee
  • Cream
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. First of all lets soak ladyfingers with coffee
  2. Then line the bottom of a baking dish with soaked ladyfingers
  3. After that we are going to put cream at the top of the ladyfingers
  4. repeat the steps
  5. Dust with cocoa powder
