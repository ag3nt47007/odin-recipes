Tomato Pasta

An easy and delicious pasta

One of the best and easiest italian dishes ever

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan chess

Steps

  1. First of all we are going to cook the pasta for 15 minutes
  2. Now lets prepare the sauce while the pasta is cooked
  3. We are going to add the tomato sauce, garlic and olive oil together in a large pan and mix them together
  4. After the pasta is cooked lets add it to the sauce pan and mix them together
  5. Finally lets add parmesan chess at the top of the pan and its ready to serve
