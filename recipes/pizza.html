Pizza

A delicious pizza with mushroom and beef

One of the best italian dishes ever

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Beef
  • Mushroom

Steps

  1. First of all we are going to prepare our dough and make a circular shape of it
  2. Then we are going to add tomato sauce on the top of it
  3. After that we going to add the onion, beef and the mushroom at the top of the tomato sauce
  4. Then we are going to put it inside the oven for about 15 Minutes
  5. Finally we are going to slice it into triangular shapes and its ready
